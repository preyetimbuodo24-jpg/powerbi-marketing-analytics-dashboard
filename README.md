# Tesla Advertising Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview

This Business Intelligence project analyzes Tesla's digital advertising performance for the Model 3/Y Automatic Garage Opener using Power BI.

The objective was to evaluate campaign effectiveness across multiple marketing channels, identify high-performing audience segments, compare regional performance, and provide data-driven strategic recommendations for future advertising investments.

---

## Project Objectives

- Clean and transform raw marketing datasets
- Build a semantic data model
- Create calculated measures and KPIs
- Analyze campaign performance
- Measure Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Compare marketing channels and geographic regions
- Evaluate audience conversion behavior
- Simulate marketing budget scenarios
- Present business recommendations based on analytics

---

## Tools & Skills Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization


---

## Dataset

The project combines four raw datasets containing:

- Campaign metadata
- Marketing performance
- Audience information
- Regional campaign metrics

Data preparation included:

- Removing duplicates
- Fixing data types
- Handling missing values
- Creating calculated columns
- Creating custom DAX measures

---

## Dashboard Sections

### Channel & Regional Performance

Analyzed:

- ROAS by marketing channel
- Regional performance comparison
- Channel-region combinations

**Key Insight**

- Instagram Australia achieved the highest ROAS.
- TikTok North America generated the strongest scalable return.

---

### Campaign Engagement

Measured:

- Engagement Rate
- Cost Per Click (CPC)

**Key Insight**

- YouTube produced the highest engagement while maintaining the lowest CPC.

---

### Audience Conversion Analysis

Compared audience segments including:

- EV Owners
- Gen Z

Metrics:

- Conversion Rate
- Cost Per Lead
- Lead-to-Purchase Rate

**Key Insight**

- EV Owners converted most efficiently.
- Gen Z had the strongest purchase intent after entering the sales funnel.

---

### Budget Scenario

A forecasting model simulated a **20% increase in TikTok advertising budget**.

Results showed:

- Purchases increased from **5,946** to approximately **7,140**
- Roughly **20% projected growth**
- No indication of declining efficiency under current assumptions

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in TikTok North America
- Maintain Instagram as a high-performing secondary channel
- Utilize YouTube for awareness campaigns
- Reduce LinkedIn advertising spend
- Develop separate marketing strategies for EV Owners and Gen Z audiences
